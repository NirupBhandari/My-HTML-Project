About

From the ‘City of Joy,’ Kolkata, Nandini Bhattacharya is a Travel Writer by profession and traveller by passion.
I love to explore places around the world, new cultures, different cuisines and all new things that one can learn outside the home.
I also loves to document my journey so that people can be inspired and travel more.
I am a nature lover and talks about sustainable tourism.
I want to make this world a good place where people can live healthy and happily.
